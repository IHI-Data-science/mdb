4.1. Forms Management¶

MosquitoDb assits with data collection through data collection forms , that are customized and readily available to fit different vector specific projects and/or experiments. Though they were previously made specifically for mosquito data collection, they are now being adapted to other vectors starting with Snails.

The available forms are filled with specific field and the user can easily add other fields that they will need in their experiment.
